About Us

Georgia Sweet

Freelance Graphic/Web Designer, Podcast Host

Originally from St. John's, Newfoundland, and currently based somewhere on Planet Earth, Georgia travels the world in a tireless search of inspiration for her next project.

Vic Vinegar

Freelance Photographer/Web Developer, Podcast Host

Vic creates websites for small to medium sized companies. He'll travel anywhere he can find sun and cheap beer - favourite spots include Central America and Eastern Africa. He occasionally stops by his home base of Fredericton, New Brunswick.
